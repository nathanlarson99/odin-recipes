Tex-Mex Pork Chops and Rice

Description

Pork Chops,Zucchini,Tomatos,Green chiles, and Corn all cooked together in a skillet. A perfect dish for any occasion, and goes well with quesadillas or rice. You could try avocados with this dish as well.

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 4 boneless pork chops, about 3/4-inch thick
  • 2 teaspoons taco seasoning spice blend
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup chopped onion
  • 1 cup coarsely chopped green bell pepper
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 (10-ounce) cans diced tomatoes and green chilies (such as Ro-telÂ®), with juice
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • freshly ground black pepper to taste
  • 1 1/4 cups chicken broth
  • 2 cups frozen corn kernels, thawed
  • 2 cups sliced zucchini
  • 1 cup uncooked rice
  • flat-leaf parsley or cilantro (optional)
  • lime slices (optional)
